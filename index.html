What We Do

We craft hand-carved sculptures that capture sporting triumphs, family treasures, and life's defining moments. Each piece is immortalised in solid 18-carat gold — a timeless work of art, cast forever in precious metal

Our Medium

Our lost-wax process melts away a detailed wax model, replacing it with molten 18ct gold to create a one-of-a-kind piece that combines timeless beauty with lasting strength

Our Inspiration

Irene Steer, won her Olympic gold in the 1912 Stockholm Olympics, when women's competitive sport was still in its infancy.

She became an icon as the first Welsh woman to win an Olympic gold, and Great Britain's inaugural gold in the women's 4x100m freestyle relay, cementing her place in both Welsh and British sporting history.

Inspired by her legacy, we dedicate ourselves to honoring our family history with our enduring commitment to excellence.
